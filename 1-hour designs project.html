What's this about?

As the name suggests, this portfolio piece aims to present a wide range of designs that are mocked-up in 1-2 hours of work (or a single sitting). These will begin with a design problem and then a breif overview of what I did to solve the issue. As such, there will be no discussion of the user-centred design process here, unlike my other portfolio works. This represents a showcase of my technical ability.

Project: Falsify

"A News App Designed for Combating Fake News"

My idea here was to design an app that aggregates all of the same stories into one page. If the user doesn't trust how one publication is representing a story, they can easily swipe between other publications (or social media's) representation of the issue. From there they can easily scroll to read the story selected.

Deisgned in 1hr 36 min on Feb 20th 2020.

Project: Take-a-Train

"An App for Train Tickets"

Ever had a train cancelled without being notified? This little piece of design focuses on how a notification would look if you had a cancelled train. I also played with a wallet-like interface for picking tickets.

Deisgned in 47 min on Nov 18th 2019.

Project: Thomas Cook.

This was a challenge given to me by a friend. Ultiamtely, my job was to update the web page with a fresh new aesthetic that the company would happily rock if they were open today.

Deisgned in 1hr 3 min on Sep 23rd 2019.
